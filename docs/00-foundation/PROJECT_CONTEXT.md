# 1. Executive Summary

WisForms is an AI-powered Form Builder and Workflow Automation Platform designed to help organizations build, manage, automate, and analyze digital forms and business workflows without writing code.

Unlike traditional form builders that primarily focus on data collection, WisForms aims to become an operational platform where forms initiate complete business processes involving approvals, automation, notifications, integrations, analytics, and AI assistance.

The long-term objective is to evolve WisForms into a modular SaaS platform capable of serving educational institutions, businesses, non-profit organizations, government departments, and enterprises.

# 3. Background

Organizations still rely heavily on:

- Paper forms
- Static PDFs
- Spreadsheet-based tracking
- Email approvals
- WhatsApp communication
- Manual data entry
- Fragmented software

These processes are often slow, error-prone, difficult to audit, and expensive to maintain.

While several commercial form builders exist, many organizations require greater flexibility, stronger workflow capabilities, localized experiences, AI assistance, and extensibility without adopting multiple disconnected systems.

WisForms addresses these needs through a unified platform.

# 10. Core Principles

WisForms will be designed around the following principles:

### Simplicity

Powerful capabilities without unnecessary complexity.

### Automation First

Reduce repetitive manual work wherever possible.

### AI Assisted

Provide contextual assistance throughout the product lifecycle.

### Extensibility

Allow integrations and future expansion without major redesign.

### Security by Design

Protect user data through robust security practices.

### Accessibility

Design for users with diverse abilities and devices.

### Performance

Deliver responsive experiences even for large-scale deployments.
